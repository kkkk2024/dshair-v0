## 申诉说明 (Appeal Description)

I am writing to formally appeal the restriction on my Airwallex account.

### 1. Business Identity

My company is **Kangde (HK) Jiankang Technology Limited**, a Hong Kong-registered company. We operate a B2B wholesale hair extensions brand called **D.S HAIR & BEAUTY** (website: dshairbeauty.co.uk), supplying 100% Remy human hair extensions to professional salons across the UK and EU. We are a Manchester-based trade supplier with 19+ years of industry experience.

### 2. Nature of Business

Our business model is strictly **B2B wholesale** — we do not sell directly to consumers. Our customers are registered salon partners who apply for a trade account before accessing pricing. All transactions are wholesale trade orders, not retail e-commerce.

The website (dshairbeauty.co.uk) serves as a trade catalogue and lead generation platform. It does not have an online checkout or shopping cart function. All orders are processed offline via invoice.

### 3. Use of Airwallex Account

We use Airwallex for:
- Receiving B2B trade payments from UK/EU salon clients
- Paying overseas suppliers (hair manufacturers)
- Multi-currency business operations (GBP, EUR, USD, HKD)

We selected Airwallex specifically for its multi-currency capabilities, which are essential for our cross-border wholesale business.

### 4. Supporting Documents Available

We are prepared to provide:
- Hong Kong Certificate of Incorporation — **Kangde (HK) Jiankang Technology Limited**
- Hong Kong Business Registration Certificate
- Director/shareholder identification documents
- Business bank statements showing trade payment history
- Supplier contracts and purchase orders
- Client trade invoices
- Website and domain ownership verification (dshairbeauty.co.uk)
- Brand trademark registration (D.S HAIR & BEAUTY)

### 5. Request

We respectfully request a review of our account status and the opportunity to provide any additional documentation required for verification. Our business is legitimate, fully registered, and operates transparently. We are committed to full compliance with Airwallex's terms of service.

Should you require any further information or documentation, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are available to provide whatever is needed to complete the verification process.

Thank you for your time and consideration.
