## Additional Recommendations

### A. Google Business Profile — B2B-Specific Strategy

Even though DS Hair Beauty is B2B, Google Business Profile is essential because:

1. **Local Search:** Salon owners search "hair extensions supplier near me" or "wholesale hair extensions Manchester"
2. **Google Maps:** Physical warehouse location adds legitimacy — clients know stock is genuinely in the UK
3. **Knowledge Panel:** A verified profile helps your business appear in the right-hand Knowledge Panel for branded searches
4. **Reviews:** Salon owners leaving reviews builds social proof for other B2B buyers

**Category Selection:** "Wholesaler" as primary. Add secondary categories like "Hair Extensions Supplier" if available. Avoid "Hair Salon" — this misleads end consumers.

### B. Industry-Specific Trade Directories

| Directory | URL | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **HJI (Hairdressers Journal International)** | https://hji.co.uk/ | UK's leading hair industry publication. Consider advertising/sponsorship. They run Salon International (the UK's biggest hair expo). Contact for trade directory/advertising options. |
| **NHBF Trade Member Directory** | https://www.nhbf.co.uk/ | Already covered above — highest priority industry listing. |
| **Professional Beauty Group** | https://professionalbeauty.group/ | Runs trade shows. Supplier/exhibitor directories available. |
| **Salon Gold** | https://www.salongold.co.uk/ | Primarily insurance for salons. Not a supplier directory — skip unless they have a partner supplier programme. |
| **HBSA (Hair & Beauty Suppliers Association)** | https://hbsa.co.uk/ | Trade association for hair & beauty suppliers in the UK. Member directory at https://hbsa.co.uk/members/. Membership benefits include industry networking and credibility. |

### C. LinkedIn Company Page — B2B Growth Strategy

LinkedIn is the #1 B2B platform. Beyond the basic company page:

1. **Personal Profile:** The business owner/founder should create a complete personal LinkedIn profile, connect with salon owners, and share industry content
2. **Content Strategy:**
   - Product showcases (new shade arrivals, quality comparisons)
   - Educational content (how to select the right extension method for different hair types)
   - Behind-the-scenes (Manchester warehouse operations, quality control process)
3. **LinkedIn Groups:** Join and participate in:
   - UK Hair & Beauty Professionals
   - Hair Extension Specialists UK
   - Salon Owners Network UK
4. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator:** Consider if budget allows (~£49.99/month) — advanced search filters to find salon owners by location, company size
